Frequently Ask Questions

How do I know if my order is confirmed ?

Upon placing an order successfully, you should receive a payment receipt from your bank or Rely as well as a separate SK Jewellery confirmation email. Alternatively, if you have registered an account, your order will be reflected as ‘Processing’.

Can i ammend my order ?

We will attempt to accommodate order changes, including billing and delivery details, prior to shipping confirmation. Do note that changes to orders may result in shipping delays. Please call us at +1 (416)-792-5215 or send us an email at inquiry@gracegems.com and quote your order number to make any amendments.

Can i cancel my order ?

We will not be able to cancel an order once payment is confirmed. You may instead, reach out to us to amend your order or make an exchange at any SK Jewellery store within 7 days from the date of receipt (if applicable).

Can i exchange the product ?

Yes, you may head down to any SK Jewellery store to make a 1-to-1 exchange within 7 days from date of receipt. Kindly bring along the merchandise in it’s original condition and packaging, along with the invoice. Sale of personalised products, special orders and damaged products are not eligible for exchange..

Do you provide cleaning services ?

We are happy to assist you in keeping your jewellery at its best condition. Do drop by our stores so we can inspect the merchandise and offer professional cleaning at no charge.

Do you provide engraving services ?

Machine engraving is available for merchandise with sufficient space for engraving. Charges will apply. Please contact any SK Jewellery store nearest to you for more information.

Do you provide resizing services ?

Resizing service is available at all SK Jewellery stores. Kindly head down to any SK Jewellery store nearest to you to find out more.
